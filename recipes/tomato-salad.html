Tomato Salad Recipe

The perfect accompanyment for the greatest lasagna recipe known to man!

A simple recipe for a perfect italian side dish.

Ingedients

  • 7 tomatoes
  • 1 small yello onion
  • 0.5 cup white sugar
  • 0.5 cup distilled white vinegar
  • 0.5 cup vegetable oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Thinly slice the tomatoes. Cut the onion in half through root end, then thinly slice into half-circles.
  2. In a large bowl, toss together tomatoes, onion, sugar, vinegar, oil, salt, and pepper. You may adjust the amounts of vinegar and sugar according to your taste. Serve at room temperature.
